Competition structure

Three rounds. One state champion.

Each grade competes independently — a Class 1 champion and a Class 8 champion. Every student has a fair shot.

01

School round

20 questions · all students

Every registered student competes. Top scorers per class advance to the district round. Results are instant.

02

District round

30 questions · school qualifiers

Top students from schools across the district compete together. Harder questions. Top scorers go to state.

03

State round

40 questions · district qualifiers

The best students from across the state compete for the title. Live leaderboard visible to all. Tiebreaker if needed.

Questions

Frequently asked

Is QuizChamp free for schools?+
Yes — registering your school and running the school round is completely free. Contact us about district and state round access.
What classes can participate?+
Class 1 through Class 8. Each class competes separately — a Class 3 student only competes with other Class 3 students.
How do students log in?+
Once your admin registers students, each gets a school code, roll number and password. They visit this site and sign in — no app download needed.
What if a student loses internet mid-quiz?+
The system distinguishes genuine network drops from deliberate tab switching. Genuine drops allow the student to rejoin — but the timer kept running server-side, so no extra time is gained. Three alerts results in removal from that round.
Where do the questions come from?+
Questions are generated by AI based on your GK book topics, then reviewed before going live. Each class gets age-appropriate questions.
Can students review their answers after?+
Yes. After every quiz, students see a full question-by-question breakdown — what they answered, whether it was correct, response time, and points earned or lost per question.
